All Loans
01

Development Loan

Long-term finance for property, construction, renovation, land purchase, and development projects.

  • Loan limit: KES 1.8 Million
  • Repayment: 1-60 months
  • Interest: 1% reducing balance/month
02

Emergency Loan

Quick access to funds for unforeseen urgent needs and emergencies.

  • Fast approval process
  • Repayment: 1-12 months
  • Interest: 1% reducing balance/month
03

School Fees Loan

Education financing to support your children's academic advancement and development.

  • Support education goals
  • Repayment: 1-12 months
  • Interest: 1% reducing balance/month

Salary Advance

Short-term advance on your salary for managing interim cash flow needs.

  • Quick disbursement
  • Repayment: 1-3 months
  • Interest: 3% reducing balance/month

Refinance/Top Up Loan

Restructure or increase your existing loan facility to meet growing financial needs.

  • Loan limit: KES 1.8 Million
