About

ACC&S SACCO in Brief

ACC&S SACCO is a member-owned financial cooperative that has provided deposit and lending services since 2006. Our operations are guided by the principles of mutual assistance, inclusiveness, and financial stability.

We are regulated by SASRA and pride ourselves on transparent governance, community focus, and a strong commitment to financial education and social responsibility.

Getting Started

How to Become a Member

Join thousands of members building wealth through ACC&S SACCO. The process is simple and straightforward.

1

Obtain Registration Form

Get your ACCs application form (free of charge). Download online or pick up from our Nairobi office.

2

Pay Registration Fee

Registration fee: Kshs 500 (Major deposit: Kshs 1,000, with bank details on reverse).

3

Share Capital Deposit

Share capital atleast 300 shares each at Kshs 20 (Kshs 6,000 minimum investment).

4

Corporate Member

Corporate member: 1,000 shares each at Kshs 20 (Kshs 20,000 minumum investment).

5

Receive Copy of Bye-laws

Obtain a copy of the bye-laws at Kshs 300 (Minimum monthly savings Kshs 1,000).
